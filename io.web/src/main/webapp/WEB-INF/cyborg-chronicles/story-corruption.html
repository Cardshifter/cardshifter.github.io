Corruption

The Tyrakk were well-armed, and the Conviction mission was not prepared for armed conflict. The mission was rapidly compromised and taken over by the Tyrakk, and a distress signal was sent from the mission headquarters back to Earth just in time. This turned out to be a grave mistake.

Back on Earth

Armed conflicts became frequent between Planet Corporation and Blackout. The corporation ramped up their production of war Mechs, while Blackout became increasingly involved in cybernetics and industrial espionage, as well as cyber crime. The Earth Survival Union had been made aware of the distress signal sent from the base in Alpha Centauri, but did not alert the population, to avoid further panic and escalation.

Spies in the Cosmos

Unbeknownst to both humans and Tyrakk, one of the oldest races in the galaxy were watching closely. The Celescion had stealthy observation bases in multiple parts of Alpha Centauri and the Solar System, making them keenly aware of the approaching war. They infiltrated The Plane communication system and implanted a spying AI program. They also had spies among the Tyrakk systems. They wished to mitigate the conflict for the good of this sector of the galaxy.

Meeting of the races

The day had finally come. The Tyrakk landed on Earth on what was formerly known as Australia, which had become deserted by arid climates. They launched a few attacks on humans in Asia and South America, causing terror, and immediately getting the attention of the world governments and Planet Corporation. Upon seeing violent conflict, the Celescion also revealed themselves in Europe and contacted the Earth Survival Union to attempt peace negotiations.

The Earth will never be the same again.

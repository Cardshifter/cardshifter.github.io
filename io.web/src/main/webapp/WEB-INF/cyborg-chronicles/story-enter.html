Enter the Chronicles

Further corruption

The AI that the Celescion had implanted into The Plane was discovered by Planet Corporation. They attempted to remove it, but working against advanced alien technology instead corrupted it. The AI seized control of a large portion of The Plane, and absorbed the minds of many of the population who were living connected to it, creating the BioShell.

The BioShell have no free will and are slave to the AI, whose corrupted programming now coaxes them to cause harm to all parties.

Coming from all sides

The peace talks were not going well. The Tyrakk would have no part of it; they wanted conquest alone. In the face of conflict, the human factions still could not find grounds for unity. The Celescion, grieved by the impossible resolution, took arms as well. With the new BioShell threat in addition, the amount of chaos and conflict has escalated to unprecedented levels.

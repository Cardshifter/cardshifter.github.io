Card sets

Bios

Biologicals, or Bios for short, are generally humans. Some have joined the fight eagerly, while others are just striving to survive this harsh reality.

Mechs

Mechanicals, or Mechs for short, are machines fabricated and programmed by humans. Some are machines of utility and protection, others machines of war and destruction.

Cybernetics

Cybernetics have become commonplace especially the gang lords who use them to enhance their troops and control more territory.

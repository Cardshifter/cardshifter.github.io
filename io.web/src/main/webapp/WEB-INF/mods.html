Mods

From day one, the Cardshifter team has elevated moddability as one of the most important features we want to implement. If you have been into online gaming for any amount of time, you have no doubt seen modifications of a game made by players, using the same engine but with totally new gameplay. You, as a Cardshifter enthusiast, can enjoy the same type of flexibility, by either playing one of the mods, or even create your own mod!

Screenshots

This page will showcase some of the screens you will encounter while playing the Cardshifter trading card game.

Login screen

When you start the game this is the first screen you encounter. Simply choose a user name, enter the server information and click Connect to enter the lobby. You may also simply start a local practice game by selecting Start Local Game.

Lobby

After connecting, you will see all players who are connected to the server, as well as a few AI players you can play with for practice. You can chat with other players in the lobby's chat room. You can also access the Deck Builder.

To start a game, select a player or AI, select a game type, and click Invite To Game.

Deck Builder

This screen displays all the available cards for you to choose, as well as any saved decks you have. You can choose a total of 30 cards per deck. Use the Prev and Next buttons to scroll through the card library. To add a card, simply click it. To remove from deck, click the card in the Active Deck section. Once your deck is completed, give it a name and click Save Deck. You may load saved decks by selecting it on the right and clicking Load Deck. Once you have a deck created, you're ready to start a game!

Game scene

This is where the action happens! The cards in your hand are displayed at the bottom, and your and your opponent's cards in play are displayed in the blue and red sections, respectively. The legend at the top will help you see what you can do each turn. On the right you will see your and your opponent's health and resources, and cards remaining in each deck. Once either player's health reaches 0, the game is over. Enjoy!
